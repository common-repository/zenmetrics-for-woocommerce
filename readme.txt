== Description ==
Zenmetrics uncovers valuable insights and marketing strategies for your online store. Business is all about people, even if it is happening online. With Zenmetrics, you will get to know your customers on a whole new level and learn how to optimize your website. Data in itself isn't worth a thing, it is what you can do with it that counts. Insights into your customers' behavior and your marketing channels will help you reach more customers and **sell more**.

This plugin lets you connect your online store with your personal Zenmetrics dashboard. You can create your account at [zenmetrics.io](https://www.zenmetrics.io/?ref=wordpress-plugin).

We are now in Beta! Join our [Public Beta Program](https://www.zenmetrics.io/?ref=wordpress-plugin) and get free access to all of our features.


= Features =
* Zenmetrics focuses on analyzing your customers' actions, not just pageviews
* New order? Zenmetrics will immediately recalculate your revenue and conversion
* Individual customer profiles and timelines as if you're seeing your customers walk through your store
* Recognize the traffic source for each visit and rank them by revenue
* Detailed activity tracking for every product in your online store
* Sales Funnel Analysis for an optimized online store

= Marketing Automation =
What's the point of having insights in your data if you can't take any actions? That's why we're building a collection of marketing automation tools right inside your analytics suite.



== Installation ==
1. Activate the plugin.
2. Go to WooCommerce > Settings and choose Zenmetrics in the top bar.
3. Fill in your API tokens and click on save changes.
4. Your data will automatically synchronized!
